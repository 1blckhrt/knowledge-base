The [SQ3R](https://www.studygs.net/sq3r-reading-method-a-comprehensive-guide-to-effective-reading/) method is a guide to effective reading.

---

## Survey

Before reading the text fully, skim the chapter's:

- titles
- headings & subheadings
- captions & visuals
- questions & study guides (if provided)
- introduction and conclusion paragraphs
- summary

---

## Question

During your skim of the text, write down questions you may have such as:

- turning titles into questions
  - Heading: "The causes of World War 1"
  - Questions: "What were the causes of World War 1?"
- end of chapter questions (if provided)
- ask yourself introspectively

---

## Read

Read the text and look for answers to the questions you wrote down.

- notice any formatted terms (such as underlined or bold)
- adjust reading speed for difficult text
- think about what you read after each section

---

## Recite

After reading a section, recite what you gained from the material.

- summarize (in your own words)
- take notes
- ask yourself the questions you created earlier

---

## Review

### Part 1

- review notes

### Part 2

- revisit the material (skim through notes and text)
- recall the answers to the questions you wrote down

### Part 3

- continuously review and recall your questions and answers

### Part 4

- create table of contents
- recite and review
